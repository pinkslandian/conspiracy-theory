Some may say I'm indecisive because of my zodiac sign and being a libra the scale may never be even. Who knows I may even change this idea. To find some type of release to let the bad energy out, and not only help myself, but humanity as a whole. Who knows, maybe one day I'll be a cool hacker stealing money from big bank men and giving it out to the poor. I'm pretty sure I'll write a poem about it one day.

♥ Finding your invisible self ♥

I know you're probably thinking what do you mean find your invisible self? I'm right here reading this, Duh! We'll when I mean invisble self, I mean your conscience. Listening to yourself, and figuring out who you really are. No matter what religion you follow, I think being spirtually aware, and being good with the universe (and yourself) will help you find your invisible self. For me, I am most in touch with myself when I get to write, or when I'm somewhere super quiet with nice scenery like the woods. (I absolutely LOVE camping.) I believe to find yourself you have get out of your comfort zone and try things you never thought you'd ever do. It's hard for a lot of people to find themselves because they're living for other people. It took me a really long time to realize that life is way too short to live for other people. There has been a lot of times where I did things that made me unhappy for the happiness of others. I've stopped wearing clothes I liked, clothes that made me feel good because of what others thought. I am still learning to live for myself. I am learning to prioritize my happiness so that I could find out who I am. I know for a fact that we are all capable of achieving what we want no matter how big or small. One day we will all reach a point in our lives where we figure out where we belong. And at peace, we'll smile.
